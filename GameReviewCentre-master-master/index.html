Purpose of this website...

The purpose of this website is to review a variety of different games on different playstation consoles. The website will also include where you could buy these games and the consoles themselves for the best price.

The website will also have a list of recommended games for a variety of different genres. Including genres such as sports, fantasy and shooter are a select few of the genres we will discuss.

There will also be a contact form which you can send directly to us with any suggestions or problems you may have found with the website. We will take on board every suggestion that we recieve and try and meet every one of your suggestions.
